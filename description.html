Realife Decisions Simulator™ Description

Realife Decisions Simulator™ is an options game.
You are given a situation and options.
Choosing the best option will make you go to the next level,
While choosing a bad one will have consequences..


Realife Decisions Simulator™ is a game about the hard things in life.
By hard things I mean things that are sad, depressing, killing from the inside!
You get my point, this game is for the most intelligent ones out there.


To play, you simply press "Start".
A situation will pop up and options too.
Click the option that you think is the best.
